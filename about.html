How it started

We’re two twenty somethings who met at an interview in New York and instantly clicked. We decided that if we both got accepted that we’d become best friends, and turns out...we did.

We moved across the country to Cleveland, OH to join startups as part of our Venture for America fellowship. We purchased plants and couldn’t believe how happy taking care of something made us. (Then we got a little carried away and bought a couple hundred, but that’s a story for a different day..)

Then, over a glass of wine celebrating a raise, voila! Jubilee Plant Box was born!

For us, being a busy working woman isn't sustainable or possible without having creative outlets like planting, painting, and simply making time to take care of ourselves.

We want others to also celebrate successes, celebrate each other, and celebrate creativity. A subscription box for plants, crafts, and self care is a great little party sent to your doorstep - join the celebration!
